Our Mission



Our mission is to provide a platform to the people of Nepal through which they can buy, sell or rent books effectively. Through this people will be able to sell their books and the reader's will be able buy them at a relatively low price. We believe this will play a huge role in enhancing reading culture in Nepal.

We are a values-driven company. Our values guide the perspective of the organization as well as our actions.
